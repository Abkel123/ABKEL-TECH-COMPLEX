Where We're Headed

Strategic Goals

Our roadmap for transforming youth potential into sustained innovation across Cameroon and beyond.

01

Expand Training Infrastructure

Build a state-of-the-art computer lab and creative studio hub in Kumba to serve 500+ learners annually.

02

Launch E-Learning Platform

Deliver accredited online courses accessible to youth across all regions of Cameroon via mobile-first platform.

03

Incubate 50 Startups by 2027

Provide mentorship, seed funding connections, and workspace for youth-led tech startups in the Southwest region.

04

Open ABKEL Primary & Nursery School

Establish a technology-integrated primary school offering cutting-edge STEM education from the earliest age.

05

Achieve International Accreditation

Partner with global certification bodies so ABKEL graduates are recognized and employable worldwide.

2030 Vision

By 2030, ABKEL TECH COMPLEX will be the leading youth empowerment and innovation hub in Central Africa — producing skilled professionals, successful entrepreneurs, and community leaders who drive sustainable development across the continent.
